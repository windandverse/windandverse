---
title: "Senses for two"
author: "Roméon"
---

I woke up in a distant land  
In a world smaller than my hands  
Before my eyes adjusted to light  
You were spellbound by my bright  

Before my ears knew noise  
I found a home in your voice  
Before my tongue came to life  
You filled my days with rife  

Before I could smell trouble  
You made all a snug muddle  
Before I could recognise touch  
Your love was my only crutch  

Before I could recall a thing  
Your lullaby was what I'd sing  
Before I could picture the sea  
You imagined a future for me  

Before I could reason  
You made me a decision  
Before I could sense  
You were my present tense  

Before I could love  
You loved for two  
I have a mom  
I'm glad it's YOU!

~ Roméon🤱 ~
