---
title: "Say Something"
author: "Roméon"
---

If the sky could speak  
I would have someone to talk to  
I'd stand at the peaks  
tell it how my day was  

It would smile with its cumulus teeth  
Give me a nimbo face when I'm sad  
We'd cry together and make it rain  
No matter how blue I feel  
It would always relate  

If time could speak  
I wouldn't ask how much I had left  

If the wind could speak  
I would ask it to shut up  

If trees could speak  
I'd ask them how it feels  
To lose all their leaves  

If the ocean could speak  
I'd ask it how to make waves  

If air could speak  
Every breath would tell a story  

If phones could speak  
I'd get the mute version  
Where disability is inability  

If the girl next to me could speak  
I would smile and call her the wind

~ Roméon🤫 ~
