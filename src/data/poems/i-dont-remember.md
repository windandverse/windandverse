---
title: "I Don’t Remember"
author: "Roméon"
---

I don’t remember my first step  
But that is when  
I learned how to walk  

I don’t remember my first word  
But that is how  
I began to talk  

I don’t remember how  
or when or why  
I thought we could  
be more than ourselves-  
be each others  
But that is where  
I learnt to unlock  
the padlock of  
my rock heart  

because nothing felt off  
So we couldn’t be  
nothing  
loving you is  
never a trade-off  
Just a trance in action  
I see the greatest version of me  
In your eyes and still  
It gets better  

How you still me  
As we are still in motion,          
my one direction        
the potion to my devotion     

I don’t remember my true love  
because they are the gift  
in the present  
making every passing moment  
Effervescent

~ Roméon🤔 ~
