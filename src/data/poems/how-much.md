---
title: "How Much"
author: "Roméon"
---

You smiled  
When I said I couldn't sleep  
Was up all night  
thinking  

If you could be anything in this world  
Would you choose to be mine?  
Call me silly but I'm unfeigned  
The way I'm falling for you is unsafe  
My breathing quivers, knees shivers  
I could have sworn I had a better chance  
At painting the sky with a brush than  
Holding your hand whenever I want  
In a trance, you were my reverie  
When I blink, I feel robbed  
For when I look at you the game's gone  
These days, even melodious chirping is  
noise  
when you speak  
like a gentle river sculpting the land  
Pristine, awe-inspiring, ethereal  
A product of god on dopamine  
I want u on me like melanin  
to the deepest ends of the calendar  
I wish you knew, I wish I knew  
Just how much, how much..!

~ Roméon🤯 ~
