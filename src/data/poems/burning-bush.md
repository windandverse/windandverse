Beating around the bush  
Feels like all I do  
I know what I mean to you  
I know how you feel  
And the role I fill  
I would be to the point  
can't wait till we're joint  

But I'm afraid  
Our friendship is sacred  
A burning bush  
My Pharaoh  
Calling me by my name  
Even if I take off my shoes  
We will fall out, burn out  
Then walk out  

But if I beat around the bush  
I can love you without being consumed  
But lately I swear  
I'd risk being ash and bones  
Than be within touch  
And out of sync with your soul  

And even if  
The promised land isn't promised  
At least I see the view  
Of the life I'll miss  
At the edge of Canaan  
Incomplete, like pain on
