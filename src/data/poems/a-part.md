---
title: "A-Part"
author: "Roméon"
---

In the beginning it was just me and her, here  
We couldn't be apart  
Couldn't be taught how to love  
Our fate written in the stars  
My reflection a blur without hers  
My light on a new moon  
Her knight in a monsoon  
Desire burning like the sun  
Hearts drowning in love  
Consumed  
too much of something  
We knew too much  
Said too little  
routine became riddle  
Passion led pain, Paying  
soul mates turned sorrow mates  
There's no good bye in forever  
nor in together  
Now, It's him. It's her. There. Where?

~ Roméon👩‍🦯🧑‍🦯‍➡️ ~
