title: "Balcony"
author: "Roméon"

the wind scream the storm speaks   
the trees dance and the leaves beat      
the clouds sob and the earth sulks       
Every breath slow and timeless         
I'm the painter, this frame is my        
canvas        
vroom! vroom! learning cars after        
English       
street lights mask the secrets of the      
city       
Planes going places I'll never see      
A drunk screaming at his wife      
or is it the other way around       
or are they both drunk?       
oh no! he's getting robbed      
I'm omniscient not kind and besides     
These bars hold me back      
Who on earth invented balustrade      
it's only a fifty foot drop     
makes me fifty six foot tall      
Not exactly top of the world      
But to see and not to care is enough      
Power      
The peace to watch the chaos unfold      
Spying, the tertiary consumer       
above the smoke and the noise      
the dotted stars form a smiling      
constellation      
Not tonight, my felo de se will have to     
wait   

~ Roméon🙄 ~
