---
title: "purple shadow"
author: "Roméon"
---


On this day five minutes ago    
I saw your purple shadow     
It’s true what they say      
blue and red make purple     
All the blood you are bleeding      
with all the tears I am crying     
I can see and feel you      
But I can’t see nor feel you      
our reflection, our own      
but our shadows unknown     
The only hope I have      
Is that you now have your light     
shadow isn’t made from dark    
And life isn’t made of dusk      

As I saw your shape     
plastered on the ground      
I knew I was late    
infected was the wound    
Then I wished      
shadows could breathe     
I wouldn’t need your bequeath    
My imagination      
became my frustration      
The half images of you      
all rushed     
all starved    
of the life we had     
and all that   
reduced to a shadow    
Purple shadow     
our life had so much color    
but the pain      
slayed my glimmer     
dulled my shimmer      
left me to simmer      

That was five minutes ago     
When you texted goodnight     
I guess I’ll see you     
tomorrow, in the afterlife  

~ Roméon💜 ~
